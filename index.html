Empowering Women Globally

Women in DeFi (WID), founded by Sarah Idahosa, has significantly impacted African women by transitioning them from Web2 to Web3 careers through education, mentorship, and financial inclusion initiatives. As of early 2026, the community has reached over 5,000 women across Africa, with over 30% of participants successfully launching blockchain projects.

What We Offer

Everything You Need to Thrive in Web3

From fundamentals to advanced DeFi strategies, our cohort-based programs are built for the next generation of women leaders.

🎓

Structured Learning

Cohort-based curricula covering blockchain fundamentals, DeFi protocols, smart contracts, and tokenomics — at your own pace with a community behind you.

🤝

Peer Mentorship

Get matched with experienced women in Web3 — founders, developers, investors — who guide your journey and open doors you didn't know existed.

🌍

Global Network

Connect with 8,000+ members across 16 chapters in Africa. Share opportunities, collaborate on projects, and build lasting professional relationships.

💼

Career Pathways

Access exclusive job boards, grants, partnerships, and startup resources curated for women building in the decentralized economy.
